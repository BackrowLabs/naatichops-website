A short history

A small dining room
with a long memory.

Welcome to Naati Chops. Discover the heart and soul of Naati style non-veg cuisine right in the heart of BTM Layout, Bangalore.

We take pride in serving you an array of delectable dishes that are rich in flavor and steeped in tradition. Our kitchen is where culinary expertise meets time-honored recipes, resulting in an unforgettable dining experience.

Whether you’re a connoisseur of South Indian cuisine or looking to explore new flavors, we invite you to savor every bite. At Naati Chops, we don’t just serve food; we share the essence of South India on your plate. Join us for an exquisite culinary journey today.
